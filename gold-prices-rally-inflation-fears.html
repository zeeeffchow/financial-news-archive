Gold Prices Rally as Inflation Concerns Resurface

December 12, 2024 | Commodities Market Report

Gold futures climbed 2.1% to $2,085 per ounce as investors sought safe-haven assets amid renewed inflation concerns.

The precious metal benefited from a weaker dollar and growing expectations that the Federal Reserve may need to maintain higher interest rates for longer.
