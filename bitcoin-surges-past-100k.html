Bitcoin Surges Past $100,000 Milestone
By Michael Chen | December 12, 2024 | Cryptocurrency Markets

Bitcoin reached a historic milestone today, surging past $100,000 for the first time as institutional adoption accelerates and regulatory clarity improves globally.

The cryptocurrency jumped 8.5% in 24 hours to trade at $102,450, driven by announcements from major financial institutions including BlackRock and Fidelity expanding their crypto offerings.
